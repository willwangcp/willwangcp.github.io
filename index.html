About Me

I'm Research Scientist at University of Scienceand Technology of China(USTC),China, working in Hefei National Laboratory for Physical Sciences at Microscale. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive models and codes.

I received my PhD in Physical Chemistry from the University of Science and Technology of China. My primary research interests lie in theoretical and computational simulations within the field of physical chemistry, encompassing but not limited to the development of electronic transport programs, investigation into the mechanisms behind photo- and electro-catalysis of two-dimensional materials, high-throughput screening of crystal structures for novel materials, and machine learning in materials simulation. Currently, I mainly utilize C++ and Python for scientific computing program development.
